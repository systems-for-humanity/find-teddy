# store listing

## app name

Find Teddy!

## short description (max 80 chars)

Pop the balls, dig out the teddy bear! A cheerful color game for toddlers.

## full description

Teddy is buried under a big pile of bouncy balls — help him out!

A friendly cowboy teddy bear calls out a color, and your child pops every
ball of that color to dig him free. Shake the phone to mix the pile, watch
the last ball of each color explode, and find Teddy to win.

- Learn colors through play: red, blue, green, yellow, purple, orange
- Real physics: balls tumble, bounce, and bury Teddy
- Shake the phone to stir the pile
- Fun voice prompts, pops, booms, and haptics
- No ads, no in-app purchases, no data collection, no internet access
- Made for toddlers: no menus to get lost in, no wrong way to play
