# Find Teddy! — privacy policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Find Teddy! is a children's game published by Systems for Humanity.

## Data collection

Find Teddy! collects no data. None.

- The app has no internet access (it does not request the network
  permission at all), so nothing ever leaves your device.
- No personal information, identifiers, usage analytics, or diagnostics
  are collected, stored, or shared.
- There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no third-party SDKs.
- The device microphone, camera, location, and contacts are never
  accessed. The only capability the app uses is vibration, for haptic
  feedback when a ball pops.

## Children's privacy

The app is designed for young children. Because it collects no data and
has no network access, there is nothing to disclose, sell, or delete
under COPPA, GDPR, or similar regulations.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be published at this
address alongside an updated app release.
